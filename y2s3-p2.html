Unwritten Destiny

Sungai Gisir Community Library · Lembah Keramat

YearYear 2
SemesterSemester 3
TypeCommunity Library
CourseDesign Studio VI
LocationLembah Keramat, KL
SiteBeside Sungai Gisir
Description

Unwritten Destiny Library is a community hub in the heart of Lembah Keramat, beside Sungai Gisir, providing spaces for reading, learning, and social interaction. Its design connects the community to the riverfront and surrounding streets.

The library is organised around a central atrium that draws the river's presence — light, sound, seasonal flood levels — into the building's core. Reading rooms step outward toward the riverbank, while community spaces open onto the adjacent street, creating a building that is simultaneously inward and engaged.

The name reflects the idea that every visitor can shape their own journey of discovery, making each experience unique. The spatial sequence offers no singular path; instead, a network of routes, terraces, and thresholds allows the building to mean different things to different people at different times.

Material choices respond to the tropical climate: deep overhangs shade the reading rooms, louvred walls allow cross-ventilation, and a planted roof terrace returns green space to the riverbank corridor.
