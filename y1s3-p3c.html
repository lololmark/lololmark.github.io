Sylvan Solace

Campus Guest Retreat

YearYear 1
SemesterSemester 3
TypeCampus Guest Retreat
CourseDesign Studio III
Description

A campus guest retreat design that draws from the surrounding natural landscape, creating a sanctuary of calm within an academic environment.

Sylvan — of the forest — names the retreat's deepest aspiration: a building that belongs to its tree canopy as much as to its occupants. Rooms step down the topography, connected by timber walkways that thread between existing trees, preserved as primary spatial elements.

Public and private zones are layered vertically through the landscape, with communal spaces opening outward to landscape terraces and private rooms oriented to filtered morning light through dense foliage.
