Sylvan Solace

Campus Guest Retreat

YearYear 1
SemesterSemester 3
TypeCampus Guest Retreat
CourseDesign Studio III
Description

Project 3 focuses on designing a single-storey campus guest retreat for short-term visitors on a 600 sqm site at Tunku Abdul Rahman University. Designed for two guests, the retreat includes spaces for sleeping, resting, dining, and working. It embraces tropical architectural principles such as natural ventilation, sun shading, and sustainable timber use. With a calm, balanced layout, the design harmoniously connects with the natural surroundings while prioritizing climate responsiveness, comfort, safety, and accessibility.
