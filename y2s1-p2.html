PAASWE2025

Booth & Bench Installation

YearYear 2
SemesterSemester 1
TypeBuilt Installation
CourseDesign Studio IV
EventPAASWE 2025
Description

A booth and bench installation designed and built for PAASWE2025, demonstrating structural ingenuity, material craft, and public space activation.

The installation moves from drawing board to physical reality through a hands-on making process — each component detailed for ease of assembly and structural integrity, each joint considered both for strength and for the visual language it contributes to the whole.

The bench and booth work together to define a semi-enclosed public zone within the exhibition, inviting visitors to pause, sit, and engage with the surrounding work. Making architecture at 1:1 scale is the most direct education in the realities of material, weight, and connection.
