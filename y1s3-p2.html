Beatflare

Wearable Architecture

YearYear 1
SemesterSemester 3
TypeWearable Architecture
CourseDesign Studio III
Description

An exploration of wearable architecture — garments and structures that blur the boundary between the body and built space, expressed through the concept of Beatflare.

Beatflare translates the pulsing energy of sound and music into a wearable structural form. The design responds to bodily movement, expanding and contracting like sound waves radiating from a source, making the wearer simultaneously performer and architecture.

The project challenges the conventional separation between skin, clothing, and shelter, proposing a fluid continuum where the human body becomes the ground floor of a personal spatial experience.
