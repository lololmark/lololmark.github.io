Neptunia House

Schematic Design — Residential

YearYear 1
SemesterSemester 3
TypeSchematic Design
CourseDesign Studio III
ProgrammeResidential
Description

Schematic design proposal for Neptunia House — a residential dwelling exploring fluid, water-inspired spatial sequences and organic structural form.

Taking its name from the aquatic genus Neptunia, the house draws on the language of water: flowing plan arrangements, pools of light, and curved surfaces that seem to respond to an invisible current. The spatial sequence mirrors the experience of moving through water — gradual, enveloping, and ever-changing in light quality.

The schematic design establishes the primary spatial relationships, structural concept, and material strategy for a contemporary tropical residence that celebrates its natural context.
