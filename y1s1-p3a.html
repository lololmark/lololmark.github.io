Precedent Studies

Library Thungnatapin School

YearYear 1
SemesterSemester 1
TypePrecedent Studies
CourseDesign Studio I
Description

Analysis and precedent study of Library Thungnatapin School, examining architectural principles, spatial organisation, and design strategies.

The study investigates how the building's spatial hierarchy, material palette, and relationship with the surrounding landscape can inform future design decisions. Key architectural principles — scale, proportion, light quality, and material honesty — are documented and analysed.

This research-based project develops critical skills for understanding built precedents as tools for generating original design proposals.
