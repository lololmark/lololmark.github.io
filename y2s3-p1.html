Site Analysis

AU5 Lembah Keramat

YearYear 2
SemesterSemester 3
TypeUrban Site Analysis
CourseDesign Studio VI
LocationAU5 Lembah Keramat, KL
Description

Site analysis of AU5 Lembah Keramat, investigating the urban fabric, community dynamics, river corridor, and opportunities for architectural intervention.

Lembah Keramat (Sacred Valley) is a dense mixed-use urban neighbourhood in Kuala Lumpur, bisected by the Sungai Gisir river corridor. The analysis maps layers of significance: historical settlement patterns, contemporary land use, community gathering nodes, and the ecological corridor of the river.

The study reveals tensions between the river's potential as public space and its current inaccessibility, informing the subsequent community library design that seeks to bridge neighbourhood and waterfront.
