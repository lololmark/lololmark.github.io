Weight of Harmony

Site Analysis — Bendang Studio & Touchstone

YearYear 2
SemesterSemester 1
TypeSite Analysis
CourseDesign Studio IV
ContextAgricultural Landscape
Description

Site analysis for Bendang Studio and Touchstone — Weight of Harmony examines the relationship between built and natural environments in the context of an agricultural landscape.

The study maps the bendang (paddy field) landscape through multiple lenses: topographic variation, seasonal water levels, existing built fabric, community patterns of use, and the poetic qualities of light across open water and cultivated earth.

The analytical title — Weight of Harmony — reflects the delicate balance between human cultivation and ecological rhythm, a tension that any architectural intervention must carefully navigate.
