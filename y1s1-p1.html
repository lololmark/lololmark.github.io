Beauty of Nature

The Vibrance

YearYear 1
SemesterSemester 1
Type3D Form Manipulation
CourseDesign Studio I
Description

This project is a three-dimensional (3D) form manipulation artwork from an animal inspiration. The animal chosen as inspiration is the Colorado Potato Beetle.

The vivid striped patterns and compact, dome-shaped form of the beetle guided the translation into a sculptural architectural composition, exploring how natural geometry can be abstracted into built form.
