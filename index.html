About

Rachel Chong
Xin Jie

CS & architecture student at TAR UMT (Tunku Abdul Rahman University of Management and Technology), Kuala Lumpur, passionate about spaces that respond to nature, community, and culture. Every project begins with listening — to the site, the people, and the light.

Projects range from sculptural three-dimensional studies to community buildings and urban schematic designs, always seeking the balance between poetic intent and structural rigour.

Particularly interested in wearable architecture, precedent studies, and narrative-driven design — where a building tells a story before a single word is spoken.
