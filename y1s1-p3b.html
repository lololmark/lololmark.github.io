Y1S1 · Project 3B · Pavilion Design

Spiral of Serendipity

Hiatus Pavilion · City Park Seremban 2

YearYear 1
SemesterSemester 1
TypePavilion Design
CourseDesign Studio I
SiteCity Park, Seremban 2
InspirationGasing (Spinning Top)
Description

Project 3B is a design proposal for a pavilion in City Park, Seremban 2, functioning as a resting place after physical activities while allowing visitors to enjoy the surrounding environment.

The inspiration is the gasing — the traditional Malaysian spinning top — whose centrifugal energy and spiralling motion are translated into the pavilion's architectural form. The structure rotates around a central axis, creating layered canopy levels that filter light and provide shade in varying degrees.

The design celebrates cultural heritage while offering a contemporary sheltered space that connects visitors to the park's natural landscape through framed views and dappled light.
