Site Analysis

TAR UMT Campus

YearYear 1
SemesterSemester 3
TypeSite Analysis
CourseDesign Studio III
LocationTAR UMT Campus, KL
Description

Comprehensive site analysis of the TAR UMT campus, examining circulation, land use, environmental conditions, and existing spatial relationships.

The study maps pedestrian and vehicular flows, solar orientation, wind patterns, existing vegetation, and building typologies across the campus. Key zones — academic, recreational, social, and transitional — are identified and their interrelationships analysed.

This analytical groundwork forms the basis for subsequent schematic design proposals that seek to enhance the campus experience through thoughtful architectural intervention.
