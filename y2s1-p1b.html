Protevora

Workers Dormitory — Bendang Studio

YearYear 2
SemesterSemester 1
TypeWorkers Dormitory
CourseDesign Studio IV
ClientBendang Studio
Project Drawings

Protevora is designed with the idea of a living vessel, where architecture shelters the earth and nature, in return, lends strength to the built form. Timber frames, living plants, and woven ceramics entwine in harmony — each sustaining, each protecting, in an endless exchange of breath and life.
