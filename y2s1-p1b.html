Protevora

Workers Dormitory — Bendang Studio

YearYear 2
SemesterSemester 1
TypeWorkers Dormitory
CourseDesign Studio IV
ClientBendang Studio
Description

Protevora is a workers dormitory design for Bendang Studio, addressing the needs of temporary residents through community spaces, shared amenities, and a connection to the working landscape.

The design rejects the institutional model of dormitory housing, instead proposing a village-like cluster of sleeping units linked by covered walkways and communal courtyards. The agricultural landscape is treated as a defining spatial element — visible from every unit, accessible from every shared space.

Material choices reflect the site's vernacular tradition: timber frames, terracotta roof tiles, and rammed earth walls that breathe with the tropical climate rather than fight it. The building belongs to the land it serves.
