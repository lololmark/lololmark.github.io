Book Analysis — A Song to Drown Rivers

As part of the design process, a selected narrative was studied and interpreted into architectural form. A Song to Drown Rivers by Ann Liang follows Xishi, whose life is shaped by duty, identity and sacrifice within constrained circumstances. The story is analysed and translated into architectural terms — turning narrative into a journey through space.
